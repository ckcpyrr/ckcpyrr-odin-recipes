Chicken Tikka Recipes

Description

Chicken Tikka is a popular Indian dish made by marinating chunks of chicken in a mixture of yogurt and aromatic spices, then grilling or baking them until perfectly cooked. The result is tender, juicy chicken with a smoky, flavorful crust.

This dish is often served as an appetizer or as part of a main meal with naan, rice, or a fresh salad. The marinade can be customized to be mild or spicy, making it a versatile favorite for both everyday meals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Skewers for grilling (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, coriander powder, oil, and salt to make the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and coat them evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1–2 hours, or overnight for best flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill, oven, or stovetop pan.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers if using a grill.
  5. Cook the chicken for 10–15 minutes, turning occasionally, until fully cooked and slightly charred at the edges.
  6. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, rice, or salad.
